Gulmarg,Beauty of Kashmir can't be compared by the whole world.

John Stephens Clock Wed 23, 2015 5

If there is heaven on earth its here its here its here. Gulmarg is one after best tourist destination around the world. the winter season is best for reaching to go and enjoying the snowfall gulmarg is proper popularly known as Switzerland of India there are sports tournaments are organised here during winter season. 1-gulmarg is known for its skiing and cable car. 2- every kind of facility available here upto 5 star hotels are available here. it is around 50 km away from Srinagar. if you are planning to go somewhere in during this winter my suggestion is to go go for gulmarg this year.

Resting at an altitudes of 8600 ft and 13600 ft respectively, Kongdoori and Apharwat are two out of the world Alpine Biosphere in Gulmarg. You can easily reach there on Cable Car (Rs. 740 to Kongdoori and Rs. 950 additional to Apharwat ). Apharwat with its snow clad 180 degree view of Pirpanjal range and snow all over provides snow activities in winter. I loved Kongdoori with its green landscaped meadows, alpine forest with different shades of green and snow capped mountains all around. You can hire pony if you so wish but real pleasure is walking around at a slow pace. Nature Unlimited. Yes, that's what you get here. Enjoy Sound of Silence, inhale pure cool air and savour unbound beauties all around. I walked almost for 90 minutes. End October clear and cold weather added to the walk. Unforgettable. Must see place. Can come again and again
